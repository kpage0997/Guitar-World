About Us

Founded in 1995, Guitar World has been a cornerstone of the musical community, providing top-notch guitars, accessories, and services to musicians of all skill levels. Our passion for music drives us to offer the best products and customer experience possible. From the moment you step into our store, you'll be greeted by knowledgeable staff who are eager to help you find the perfect instrument or accessory.

At Guitar World, our mission is to foster a love for music by providing high-quality guitars and accessories, exceptional repair services, and comprehensive lessons. We believe that music has the power to bring people together and inspire creativity, and we are dedicated to supporting musicians on their musical journeys.
